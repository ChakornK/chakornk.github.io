A word guessing game where everyone except one person knows the word. Everyone gives 1-word clues and tries to find who the imposter is.

Players can choose from different categories of words.

![Set up](/projects/imposter/1.png)
![Categories](/projects/imposter/2.png)

When the game starts, players tap on the card with their name to view the word.

![Player cards](/projects/imposter/3.png)
![Imposter](/projects/imposter/4.png)
![Word](/projects/imposter/5.png)

After everyone has viewed their card, discussion starts and players try to figure out who the imposter is.

![Discussion](/projects/imposter/6.png)

The word is revealed after the discussion ends and the imposter is eliminated.

![Win](/projects/imposter/7.png)

## Tech stack

- **Frontend**: Vue, Tailwind, Motion
